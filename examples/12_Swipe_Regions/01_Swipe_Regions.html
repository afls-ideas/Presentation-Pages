Swipe Region Control Demo

defineNoSwipeRegion

Creates a rectangular region where swipe gestures are disabled. Useful for interactive elements like carousels, sliders, or drawing areas.

SYNTAX
PresentationPlayer.defineNoSwipeRegion( regionId, x, y, width, height )

QUICK PRESETS

Mobile only. Coordinates are in pixels relative to the presentation viewport. Regions persist until explicitly destroyed or the page unloads.

destroyNoSwipeRegion

Removes a previously defined no-swipe region, re-enabling swipe gestures in that area.

SYNTAX
PresentationPlayer.destroyNoSwipeRegion(regionId)


VISUAL PREVIEW

Red zones show active no-swipe regions. Swipe gestures inside these areas will not trigger page navigation.
